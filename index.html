Thoughts on Web Accessibility

In recent years, access to the internet has become a need rather than a want. People use it for communication and work, as well as entertainment. 13% of people have a disability, the range of severity for is large however it is important that we create accommodations for as many people as possible. We see these accomodations in features like voice to text, color blind filters, and many more. Depending on your target audience, you may even create features that prevent the user from getting "lost" on your site. Increased accessibility is great for users, but buisness owners should also be considerate of accessibility because it helps user rention high.
